9. Assumptions & Limitations

  • Dwell time is measured only for containers with both gate-in and gate-out timestamps.
  • Clustering assumes Euclidean distance in standardized feature space.
  • Forecasting models assume historical patterns continue.
  • Storage cost calculations depend on the accuracy of the tariff configuration provided.
  • Yard utilization is computed from container records; physical constraints are not modelled.
  • This skill analyzes containerized cargo only. General/breakbulk cargo requires separate methodology.
  • Future enhancements: BAPLIE integration, equipment utilization correlation, real-time gate scheduling via MCP.
